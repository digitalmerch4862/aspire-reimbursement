Aspire Reimbursement Form  ·  How to Use
Welcome
This is the Aspire Reimbursement Form. Let's walk through how to complete and submit a reimbursement request.
Step 1 · Make a Copy
Open the File menu and select Make a copy. Always work from your own copy, never the master template.
Step 2 · Open the Panel
When your copy opens, a script runs automatically and the Reimbursement panel appears.
Step 3 · Solo or Group
Choose Solo for one person, or Group for several. Set the Date From and Date To, then enter the Location.
Step 4 · Add Members
For each member, enter the Staff Name, the YP Name, and the Amount. The Live Preview builds your form in real time.
Step 5 · Add More Rows
Click Add Group Row to include more participants. Each new row appears instantly in the preview.
Step 6 · Review
Once everyone is listed, review the staff names, YP names, and amounts against the live preview.
Step 7 · Sync & Export
Click Sync to Sheet to save, then Export Preview to JPEG to download your finished Group Request Form.
